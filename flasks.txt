Flask, Draught, Carafe, Vial, Ampoule - Common Potions
Grail, Chalice, Font, Goblet - Magic Potions

(Flask, Draught and Carafe are normal potions)
(Vial and Ampoule are 'lesser' potions)
(Chalice is Vial + Flask)
(Grail is Vial + Draught)
(Goblet is Ampoule + Draught)
(Font is extended Vial)
(Monstrance is extended Ampoule)

Life:
	Life Flask: flat HP over time
	Life Draught: %HP over time
	Life Vial: flat HP at once
	Grail of Life: flat HP at once, %HP over time
	Chalice of Life: flat HP at once, flat HP over time
	Font of Life: flat HP over extended time

Mana:
	Mana Flask: flat Mana over time
	Mana Draught: %Mana over time
	Mana Vial: flat Mana at once
	Chalice of Mana: flat Mana at once, flat Mana over time
	Grail of Mana: flat Mana at once, flat Mana over time
	Font of Mana: flat Mana over extended time

	Grail of Rejuvination: flat HP/Mana at once; % HP/Mana over time
	Font of Rejuvination: flat HP/Mana over extended time
